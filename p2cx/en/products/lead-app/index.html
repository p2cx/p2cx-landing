Lead App

Capture leads in two minutes – at the trade fair, by voice. Scan the badge, talk into it for a moment, done. The AI fills the CRM, you keep selling.

The problem

Filling in paper forms at the trade fair, typing them up in the evening, following up two weeks later – by then the hot leads have long gone cold.

What it does

Badge & QR scan
Scan the visitor badge – name, company and job title are right there.
Talk instead of type
Talk for 90 seconds – the AI spots buying intent, product interest and the next step.
The questionnaire fills itself
Event-specific questions are pre-filled from your note – you confirm in 30 seconds.
Straight into the CRM
Connects to Dynamics 365, duplicates are detected and linked – no double entry.
Offline-first
Works even when the trade fair Wi-Fi gives up. Syncs the moment you're back online.
Sorted by Monday morning
A “hot / warm / cold” overview plus prepared next steps – follow-up starts days earlier.

How it works

Visitor comes to the stand → scan the badge → talk briefly about what it's about → AI suggests a rating and the next step → save (20 seconds). By Monday it's all sitting neatly in the CRM, prioritised.

Secure & GDPR

Login via Azure AD, AI exclusively through Langdock (EU), data never leaves the EU. The original audio isn't archived – only the transcription counts.
